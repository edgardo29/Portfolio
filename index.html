Google Chrome Extension

Developed an extension for Chrome that allows the user to store any webpage into it and save it for later access whenever needed without having to go look for it in the web again.

Budget Tracker

This application helps you stay on top of your finances so you can better see where your capital is going. It's a great management tool to keep track of your income and expenses on the go.

Crystal Collector

In this game, a player must match a random number given through the selection of crystal balls that contain hidden numbers. After selecting a ball, a number is revealed and added to the total score. The player wins if their score matches the random number, but loses if it goes over.

Word Guess

Created a game that requires the player to guess a random word. The player will only get a certain number of guesses to form the word in order to win.
